01 — the studio

One room. One chair. Your skin only.

Rose Skin Studio is the practice of Jasmeet — "Jaz" to her clients — a licensed esthetician who built a private Brampton studio around a simple idea: skin responds to attention, not assembly lines.

Every visit begins with a real conversation about your skin, its history and its goals. Every step is explained as it happens — the product, the purpose, the aftercare — so you leave educated, not just glowing.
