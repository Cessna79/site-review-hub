Our story

A home kitchen that Brampton fell for.

Crumble Cake Factory started with one baker, Gurpreet, and a simple belief: a celebration cake should taste as good as it looks — and it should be fresh. That's why nearly every cake leaves the kitchen the same day it's baked, often within an hour or two of pickup.

Over the years, that care has earned a shelf of five-star reviews and a stream of regulars who order for every birthday, anniversary and festival. Fair prices, real customization, and cakes that aren't drowning in sugar. That's the whole recipe.
