FAQ

Questions people ask us every week.

How much does it cost to work with Immigraze?
Fees depend on the program and how much representation you want. We quote a fixed fee in writing before you commit — no hourly surprises, and government fees are always listed separately so you can see exactly where your money goes.
Can you guarantee my application will be approved?
No — and you should walk away from anyone who says yes. Decisions belong to IRCC alone. What we can do is make sure your application is complete, accurate, well-argued and submitted under the right program, which is where most preventable refusals happen.
I don't live in Canada. Can you still represent me?
Yes. Almost every IRCC application is filed online, and we work with clients worldwide by video call, WhatsApp and secure document sharing — in your time zone. Call us at 647 564 0805 or email immigraze@gmail.com to set up a time.
My application was refused before. Is it hopeless?
Usually not. A refusal is information: we obtain the officer's notes, find out exactly why it happened, and decide whether to reapply, respond or pursue another pathway. Many of our strongest approvals started as someone else's refusal.
How do I know you're not a "ghost consultant"?
Check — don't take our word for it. Every Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant is listed on the CICC's public register with their licence number and standing. Ours is in the footer of every page, linked directly to the register.
What happens after I submit the free assessment?
A licensed consultant reviews your profile personally and replies within one business day with an honest read on your options — including, when it's the truth, "not yet, and here's what would change that."
