Who We Are

A Brampton firm built on trust, not volume

Tiwana Law Chambers Professional Corporation serves individuals, families and business owners across Brampton and the Greater Toronto Area. We are deliberately a boutique practice: every client works directly with their lawyer, every document is explained clearly, and every deadline is treated as our own.

Whether you are closing on your first home, sponsoring a family member, planning your estate or launching a business, we bring the same standard of care — meticulous preparation, honest advice and steady communication from the first consultation to the final signature.

  • Direct access to your lawyer — not a call centre
  • Plain-language explanations of every document you sign
  • Clear, upfront fee estimates with no surprises
  • Services available in Punjabi, Hindi and English

"Our measure of success is simple: when the file closes, our client understands exactly what happened, why it happened, and what it cost — and would trust us again without hesitation."

That standard shapes how we prepare every transaction, application and agreement that leaves this office.

Practice Areas

Focused expertise for life's biggest decisions

We concentrate on the legal matters that matter most to families and business owners in our community.

I.

Real Estate Law

Purchases, sales, refinances and title transfers — handled with the diligence a closing deserves. We coordinate with your lender, agent and the other side so your deal closes smoothly and on time.

II.

Immigration Law

Guidance through applications from initial consultation to final approval — with responsive updates, careful documentation and honest assessments of your options at every stage.

III.

Family Law

Separation agreements, divorce, parenting arrangements and support matters — handled with sensitivity, discretion and a focus on protecting what matters most to you.

IV.

Wills, Estates & Powers of Attorney

Protect your family and your wishes with a properly drafted will, powers of attorney for property and personal care, and practical estate planning advice.

V.

Notary & Commissioner Services

Fast, accurate notarizations, certified true copies, affidavits, statutory declarations, consent letters and invitation letters — often completed the same day.

VI.

Business & Corporate Law

Incorporations, purchase and sale of a business, commercial leases and shareholder agreements — legal structure that supports your ambition instead of slowing it down.

VII.

Contracts & Agreements

Drafting and independent legal advice on agreements of all kinds, so you understand exactly what you are signing before you sign it.

How We Work

From first call to final signature

A clear, predictable process — because legal matters are stressful enough without guesswork.

STEP 01

Consultation

We listen first. You explain your situation; we outline your options, realistic timelines and what it will cost.

STEP 02

Preparation

We gather documents, review every detail and prepare your file meticulously — catching issues before they become problems.

STEP 03

Communication

You're never left wondering. We keep you updated at each milestone and explain every document in plain language.

STEP 04

Completion

We close the file properly — signed, registered, reported — and remain available for anything that comes after.
